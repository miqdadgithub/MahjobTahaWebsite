About the Archive

Learn about the mission behind this digital repository and the team preserving Professor Taha's legacy.

This digital archive is dedicated to preserving and sharing the intellectual legacy of Professor Mahjoub Taha, an eminent Sudanese physicist whose contributions to theoretical physics have inspired generations of scientists.

Our mission is to make Professor Taha's extensive body of work accessible to researchers, students, and physics enthusiasts worldwide, ensuring that his insights continue to advance scientific understanding.

The archive includes academic publications, lecture recordings, and personal notes spanning over four decades of groundbreaking research in quantum mechanics, particle physics, and cosmology.
